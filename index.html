Our Journey & History

Established in 2012, Somproperty began with a single vision: to transform the real estate landscape of Somaliland by introducing transparency, professionalism, and global standards to the local market.

Over the past decade, we have evolved from a small brokerage firm into a comprehensive real estate powerhouse, specializing in construction, architectural design, and strategic consultancy. We've played a pivotal role in the urban development of Hargeisa and beyond.
